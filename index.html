About Us

FLYSTORE is an ecommerce website platform for various products from different categories such as Clothing, Food, Footwear, Electronics, Home-appliances, Mobiles, Grocery, Cosmetics etc. We believe in providing the best quality products in affordable ranges for our users.Flystore keep on enhancing the products according to users need and demand.We pick up our products from local areas where people have ideas but do not have the privilege to showcase their art on a platform like similar. Website displays all the lists of products with their respective prices. In addition, the system allows managing product brands too. There are two categories for login on this website: Admin and Customer.In an overview of this web application, the customer has to register in order to perform various actions. A customer can look for products, add products to the cart, add products to a wishlist. Each product contains its own description, features, and number of photos. Talking about the checkout procedure, the customer can enter coupon codes for discounts. As soon as he/she passes through checkout procedures, the customer has to manage the order from the account section.
